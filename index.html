Introduction

Despite the recent ceasefire between Hamas and Israel, Palestinians in Gaza continue to face a dire humanitarian crisis that necessitates sustained international assistance. This website provides support, verified information, and educational resources for understanding and aiding Palestine.

Even with a ceasefire, the effects of prolonged conflict remain devastating. Many families have lost their homes, access to clean water and electricity is limited, and essential infrastructure has been severely damaged. Food insecurity is widespread, and hospitals are overwhelmed. Children and vulnerable communities continue to face psychological trauma and disrupted education. Because of these challenges, ongoing awareness, aid, and solidarity are crucial to support recovery and help rebuild lives in Gaza and the West Bank.

Verified Organizations

These organizations provide trusted, transparent, and effective humanitarian aid to Palestinians in need.

  • Palestine Children’s Relief Fund (PCRF) — provides medical care and humanitarian relief to children in Palestine.
  • UNRWA — offers education, healthcare, and emergency aid to Palestinian refugees.
  • Medical Aid for Palestinians (MAP) — supports health and dignity for Palestinians living under occupation.
  • ANERA — delivers food, education, and healthcare across Gaza, Jordan and Lebanon.

Current Situation

In Gaza, over two million people live under severe blockade conditions. Rebuilding efforts are slow due to restrictions on materials and the destruction of essential infrastructure. Hospitals continue to face shortages of fuel, medicine, and clean water. Displacement remains a major issue, with many families living in temporary shelters or overcrowded conditions.

International efforts are ongoing to bring humanitarian supplies into Gaza, but access remains limited. Education systems, health services, and employment opportunities have been disrupted, leaving many people dependent on aid for survival.

Educational Resources

Understanding the history and current reality of Palestine is essential to meaningful advocacy. These materials offer verified information, documentaries, and reading guides for learning more.

  • B’Tselem — An Israeli-run human rights organization documenting the reality of occupation and the horrors Gaza is going through.
  • Al Jazeera Palestine Coverage — continuous coverage from the ground.
  • Institute for Middle East Understanding (IMEU) — provides expert analysis and fact sheets.

How You Can Help

There are many ways to contribute, from raising awareness to supporting aid organizations directly.

  • Donate to verified humanitarian organizations listed above.
  • Share verified information and counter misinformation online.
  • Support Palestinian creators, journalists, and businesses.
  • Attend local solidarity events or fundraisers.
  • Contact your representatives and advocate for humanitarian aid and accountability.
